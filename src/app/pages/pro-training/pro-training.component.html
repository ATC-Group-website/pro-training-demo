1. Professional Financial Accountant (PFA)

PFA is designed to develop your knowledge of accounting standards and practices. PFA body of knowledge will effectively enhance your understanding of the underlying accounting concepts, principles and their practical application.

2. Certified Management Accountant (CMA)

Gain a globally recognized certification that equips you with the expertise needed to manage finance functions and strategic management.

3. Diploma in International Financial Reporting (DipIFR):

Enhance your knowledge of international financial reporting standards (IFRS) and improve your career prospects in the accounting and finance industry.

4. Advanced Diploma in International Taxation (ADIT):

Specialize in international taxation with a focus on key areas such as transfer pricing, VAT, and double tax treaties.

5. Financial Modeling and Analyst Association (FMAA):

This prestigious certification is designed for professionals seeking to deepen their expertise and enhance their credentials. The FMAA Certificate covers key areas such as financial analysis, risk management, and strategic planning, equipping you with the skills needed to excel in the industry.

6. TAX:

This program equips professionals with essential skills in income tax, VAT, payroll, stamp, and real estate taxes, along with training on ETA submissions, tax inspections, and electronic invoicing.
