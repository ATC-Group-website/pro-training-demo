Tailored and Specialized Training Courses

we offer specialized courses for individuals and customized corporate training to meet your business's unique needs. Our programs cover a range of topics to enhance skills, boost productivity, and address team skill gaps. Contact us for a personalized consultation to design the ideal course for your team.
Here are our current courses:

1. Professional Financial Accountant (PFA)

PFA is designed to develop your knowledge of accounting standards and practices. PFA body of knowledge will effectively enhance your understanding of the underlying accounting concepts, principles and their practical application.

2. Certified Management Accountant (CMA)

Gain a globally recognized certification that equips you with the expertise needed to manage finance functions and strategic management.

3. Diploma in International Financial Reporting (DipIFR):

Enhance your knowledge of international financial reporting standards (IFRS) and improve your career prospects in the accounting and finance industry.

4. Advanced Diploma in International Taxation (ADIT):

Specialize in international taxation with a focus on key areas such as transfer pricing, VAT, and double tax treaties.

5. Financial Modeling and Analyst Association (FMAA):

This prestigious certification is designed for professionals seeking to deepen their expertise and enhance their credentials. The FMAA Certificate covers key areas such as financial analysis, risk management, and strategic planning, equipping you with the skills needed to excel in the industry.

6. Taxation Course (CTPro):

This program equips professionals with essential skills in Corporate Tax, income tax, VAT(value added tax), payroll, stamp, and real estate taxes, along with training on ETA submissions, tax inspections, and electronic invoicing.

7. Cost:

Enroll in this course to gain expert insights into making your company's cost structure transparent, calculating costs, and mastering product costing methods. Learn to choose the best costing system for your company's needs and streamline financial decision-making.

8. Egyptian Accounting Standards (EAS):

The Egyptian Accounting Standards (EAS) course offers essential knowledge of Egypt's accounting framework, tailored for accounting professionals, finance managers, auditors, and business owners to ensure regulatory compliance and accurate financial reporting.

9. E-invoice & E-receipt:

This course equips professionals with the skills to implement Egypt's digital invoicing system, enabling compliance with government regulations and enhancing business efficiency amidst the shift to electronic processes.
